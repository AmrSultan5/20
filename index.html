Rules

Welcome to THE TREASURE HUNT!

In the treasure hunt game, click the Play button to start, and you'll be taken to a new page where you'll receive a hint about the hidden present. Based on the hint, make your guess. If your guess is correct, a map will show the exact location of the present; if it's incorrect, you can keep trying until you get it right. The goal is to continue solving hints and finding all the presents hidden in the game. Enjoy the adventure and have fun discovering the treasures!
